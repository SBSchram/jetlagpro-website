Privacy Policy

JetLagPro for Android (Google Play)

Effective July 8, 2026

This policy applies to the JetLagPro Android app on Google Play. It is a travel guide for timed acupressure. The app is informational and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment.

What stays on your device

The guide works without an account. The app stores trip and preference data locally on your phone, including:

  • Destination and schedule for trips you start
  • Which points you marked during a trip
  • Notification and display preferences
  • Whether you finished the welcome and points instructions
  • Recent destination searches

Uninstalling the app removes this local data from your device.

Anonymous device code

The app creates a random code (shown in the Info tab as your device ID). It is not your name, email, or phone number. The code helps label trips on your device and, if cloud sync is enabled, in our product database.

Optional cloud trip history

Cloud backup is on by default. Turn off Cloud backup of trips in the app Info tab if you do not want completed trips synced to Google Firebase (Firestore) in project jetlagpro-android. When sync is on, we upload only:

  • Destination airport code (three letters, e.g. LHR)
  • Trip start and completion times
  • Count of points marked (a number, not which specific points)
  • General time-zone / travel-direction summary fields used by the guide
  • App version and platform (Android)

Data is tied to your anonymous device code, not to a named account. Transfers use encrypted connections (HTTPS).

Delete your data

Tap the ✕ next to any trip on My Data or in Past trips to remove it from your device. If cloud backup is enabled, that trip is also deleted from Firebase. Turning off cloud backup stops future uploads only — it does not remove trips already stored in the cloud; use ✕ on each trip or delete all trips you want removed.

Uninstalling the app removes all local trip and preference data from your phone.

What we do not collect

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or mailing addresses
  • Account logins or contact lists
  • GPS location or continuous location tracking
  • Health records or advertising identifiers

Cloud sync stores a count of points marked, not which specific acupressure points you stimulated.

How we use data

  • To run the guide: schedules, reminders, and trip history on your device
  • To restore trip history when cloud sync is on
  • To improve the product in aggregate (anonymous usage patterns)

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or use it for cross-app tracking.

Data retention

On your device: Trip history, preferences, recent searches, and your anonymous device code stay on your phone until you delete individual trips (✕), clear recent destinations, or uninstall the app. Uninstalling removes all local data.

In the cloud (optional): If cloud backup is on, completed trip summaries are stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) until you delete that trip with ✕ in the app or email us at info@jetlagpro.com to request cloud deletion. Turning off cloud backup stops future uploads only; it does not remove trips already stored in the cloud.

We do not delete cloud trip data on a fixed schedule. We keep cloud backups only as long as needed to provide sync and trip history, or until you delete them or ask us to delete them.

Google Play purchases: If you buy in-app products, Google processes payment and may retain purchase records under Google's policies. We keep only what Google provides to verify your purchase (for example, subscription status).

Notifications

The app schedules local reminders for acupressure points. Notification permission is optional. We do not use notifications to collect personal information.

Google Play purchases (when offered)

If you buy in-app products or subscriptions, payment is processed by Google Play. We receive purchase status from Google to unlock features. We do not receive your full payment card number. Google's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

Children

The app is intended for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Policy updates

We may update this page from time to time. The effective date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
