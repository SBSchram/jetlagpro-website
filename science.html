What Does Science Tell Us?

  • We all have our own circadian rhythm - our personal 24-hour clock.
  • This clock tells us when to feel sleepy, when to be alert, and regulates many other body functions.
  • Even our individual cells have their own timekeepers.
  • Recent research suggests acupuncture and acupressure may influence the body's internal clock.
