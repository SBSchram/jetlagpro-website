Data Transparency Toolkit

  • Data Analysis — View trip outcomes and statistics with device authentication filtering.
  • Audit Log — Every database operation is recorded. Nothing can be changed without a trace.
  • Verify Data Integrity — Confirm that data hasn't been tampered with and comes from legitimate devices.

How We Prevent Data Tampering

Immutable Archive: Every audit entry is written to two places simultaneously—Firestore (for real-time viewing) and Google Cloud Storage (permanent, unchangeable record). The GCS archive is locked for 10 years and cannot be modified, even by us.

gs://jetlagpro-audit-logs/audit-logs/

Device Authentication: Starting November 8, 2025, every trip record includes a cryptographic signature proving it came from a legitimate iOS device with our app installed.

These safeguards ensure data integrity from collection through analysis. You can verify both independently using the tools above.
