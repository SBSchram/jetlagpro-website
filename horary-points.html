The Chinese Organ Clock in Everyday Terms

Chinese medicine describes a 24-hour cycle where 12 different organ systems take turns being "on duty." Each organ system is tied to a specific meridian. And each meridian has one ideal acupressure point and a second near-ideal substitution point.


JetLagPro knows the "on-duty" meridian for your destination and shows you the best point you can realistically reach. Massaging these points during your journey tells your body to time-shift to the destination time. This helps your body acclimate to the new time zone.

Ideal vs Cramped-Seating Points

Five of the ideal points are on the feet or behind the knees. In a cramped seat, those points can be hard or awkward to reach. So we substitute an accessible point.

JetLagPro always targets the on-duty meridian. With Cramped seating points on at the top of the Info tab, the app shows the accessible points. Turn it off to see the ideal points. Either way, you still get the benefit.
