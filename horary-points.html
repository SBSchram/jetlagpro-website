The 12 Horary Points

One horary acupressure point for each two-hour window, aligned to your destination time.

JetLagPro shows the ideal horary point for the active window—or a reachable alternative when you are in a tight seat.

What are horary points?

Timed acupressure for travel uses twelve horary points—one traditional point per two-hour block on the twenty-four-hour schedule. JetLagPro follows destination time, not home time, and reminds you which point to press for the window you are in now.

The two-hour schedule

The wheel shows the day divided into twelve two-hour slices. Each slice has one horary point.

Ideal horary points vs. cramped-seat alternatives

Five of the classical horary points are on the feet or behind the knees—hard to reach in a middle seat. The app can show reachable alternatives on the same two-hour window instead of skipping that window entirely.

In the Info tab, turn Cramped seating points on for those substitutes. Turn it off when you have room for the ideal horary points. Timing stays the same either way.
