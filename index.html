The 12 Horary Points

Each point is strategically located and has specific functions that help your body adjust to new time zones.

Lung-8 (LU-8)

3-5 AM

Location: Find the depression about 1 inch above the wrist just inside the bone.

Stimulation: Press into the point and rub it deeply with firm pressure. If you feel you are pressing against the bone, you have it right!

Large Intestine-1 (LI-1)

5-7 AM

Location: This point is on the thumb side of the index finger, at the top corner of the nail.

Stimulation: Press into this point and rub it with firm pressure for about a minute.

Stomach-36 (ST-36)

7-9 AM

Location: About 3 inches below the kneecap between the shin bone and the fibula. It may be sore when you press into it.

Stimulation: Lean down and firmly massage the muscle with your fingers pressing into the flesh.

Spleen-3 (SP-3)

9-11 AM

Location: Along the inside edge of your foot where a big bump starts before you reach the big toe.

Stimulation: Use your thumb to press back and forth into this point. It should feel like you are rubbing into the bone.

Heart-8 (HT-8)

11 AM-1 PM

Location: On the palm of your hand, find the big crease. The point is where your pinky touches the crease between the bones.

Stimulation: Use your index finger or knuckle to press this point between the bones. Press firmly and rub in an up and down motion.

Small Intestine-5 (SI-5)

1-3 PM

Location: Bend your elbow so your thumb points to your nose. Find the depression on the pinky side where the arm bone meets the wrist.

Stimulation: Use your index finger to press into this point while moving your hand away and to your body.

Bladder-66 (BL-66)

3-5 PM

Location: On the outside edge of the foot where the small toe meets the larger bone.

Stimulation: Use your index or middle finger to rub into the base of the big bone. It may be sore so be both firm and gentle.

Kidney-10 (KI-10)

5-7 PM

Location: While sitting, find the sore spot at the top of your bottom leg as shown in the video.

Stimulation: Rub your thumb around this area. It will be sore so don't press too hard. Apply firm pressure with a press and release method.

Pericardium-8 (PC-8)

7-9 PM

Location: Find the place on the long crease on your palm where your middle finger touches it.

Stimulation: Press between the bones with your index finger, thumb or knuckle. Press firmly and rub back and forth.

San Jiao-6 (SJ-6)

9-11 PM

Location: With your hand facing down, raise your fingers to find your wrist crease. Put four fingers on the wrist crease. The point is above the top finger, between the two arm bones.

Stimulation: Use your index finger or thumb to press between the bones and into the point.

Gall Bladder-41 (GB-41)

11 PM-1 AM

Location: With your foot on your knee. The point is between the small and fourth toe at the top of the first depression you find when moving up from the toes.

Stimulation: Press into the depression and rub up and down while maintaining a steady pressure.

Liver-1 (LIV-1)

1-3 AM

Location: On the big toe, on the inside edge, about 0.1 inch from the corner of the nail.

Stimulation: Use your index finger to press into this point and rub it around with firm pressure.
