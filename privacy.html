Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy

Effective Date: July 24, 2025

1. App Data Collection

During Beta Testing Period: We collect anonymous usage data to improve the app and conduct research on jet lag management effectiveness.

Post-Beta Release: JetLagPro will operate entirely on your device without requiring internet connectivity and will not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Beta Testing Data Collection

  • Anonymous usage statistics (app opens, feature usage)
  • Anonymous trip completion data (destination, time zones crossed)
  • Anonymous survey participation data
  • No personal identifiers, names, or contact information

2. Research Survey Data Collection

We collect anonymous survey data for research purposes through our optional research survey. Participation is entirely voluntary.

Anonymous Survey Data

  • Survey responses about jet lag symptoms and recovery
  • Basic demographic information (age range, travel experience)

3. How We Use Information

Anonymous survey responses are used solely for research purposes to evaluate the effectiveness of chronoacupuncture for jet lag management.

4. Data Storage and Security

Anonymous survey responses are securely stored in Google Firebase Firestore and cannot be linked back to individual users.

5. Third-Party Services

JetLagPro is distributed through Apple's App Store. We do not share any data with Apple beyond what is required for app distribution. Survey data is stored using Google Firebase Firestore, which provides enterprise-grade security and complies with applicable data protection standards.

6. Your Rights

Since all data collection is anonymous, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Beta testing data and survey responses are anonymous and cannot be attributed to specific individuals.

7. Children's Privacy

JetLagPro is not intended for children under 13. No personal information is collected from any users.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app with an updated effective date.
