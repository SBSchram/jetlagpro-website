Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 24, 2025

1. App Data Collection

JetLagPro operates entirely on your device without requiring internet connectivity. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information during normal use.

App Usage - No Data Collection

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage data is tracked
  • No location data is accessed
  • No analytics are performed

2. Research Survey Data Collection

We collect anonymous survey data for research purposes through our optional research survey. Participation is entirely voluntary.

Anonymous Survey Data

  • Survey responses about jet lag symptoms and recovery
  • Basic demographic information (age range, travel experience)
  • Survey completion timestamp
  • Anonymous survey code (no personal identification)

What We Don't Collect in Surveys

  • No names, email addresses, or personal identifiers
  • No location data or travel details
  • No IP addresses or device information
  • No tracking across websites or apps

3. How We Use Information

App Usage: Since no data is collected from app usage, no information is used, stored, or processed by our systems.

Survey Data: Anonymous survey responses are used solely for research purposes to evaluate the effectiveness of chronoacupuncture for jet lag management. Data is analyzed in aggregate to advance scientific understanding.

4. Data Storage and Security

App Data: All app functionality occurs locally on your device. No data is stored on external servers or transmitted to third parties.

Survey Data: Anonymous survey responses are securely stored in Google Firebase Firestore for research analysis. This data cannot be linked back to individual users and is accessible only to authorized research personnel.

5. Third-Party Services

JetLagPro is distributed through Apple's App Store. We do not share any data with Apple beyond what is required for app distribution. Survey data is stored using Google Firebase Firestore, which provides enterprise-grade security and complies with applicable data protection standards.

6. Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. Survey responses are anonymous and cannot be attributed to specific individuals.

7. Children's Privacy

JetLagPro is not intended for children under 13. No personal information is collected from any users.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app with an updated effective date.
