Privacy Policy for JetLagPro

Effective Date: July 16, 2025

JetLagPro ("we," our,us) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal InformationJetLagPro is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal personal information:

  • Destination Information: When you select a destination airport, we store the airport code and timezone information locally on your device
  • App Usage Data: Basic usage statistics to improve app functionality
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app compatibility

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal health data
  • Medical information
  • Location data (beyond airport selection)
  • Contact information
  • Payment information (app is free)

    • How We Use Your Information

    We use the information we collect to:

    • Provide App Functionality: Calculate optimal acupressure point timing based on your destination
    • Improve User Experience: Analyze app usage to enhance features and performance
    • Technical Support: Troubleshoot issues and provide customer support
    • App Updates: Send notifications about app updates and new features
    Local Storage
  • All personal data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not transmit personal information to external servers
  • Data is encrypted using iOS security features

    • Data Retention

  • App data is retained only as long as you use the app
  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • No data is retained after app deletion

    • Third-Party Services

    Apple Services
  • Apple Watch Integration: Uses Apple's WatchConnectivity framework
  • Push Notifications: Uses Apple's notification services
  • App Store: Uses Apple's App Store for distribution

    • Your Rights and Choices

    Access and Control
  • View Your Data: All data is stored locally and visible within the app
  • Delete Your Data: Uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Opt Out: Disable notifications and analytics in app settings

    • Children's Privacy

    JetLagPro is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

    Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
