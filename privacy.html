Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy

Effective Date: May 17, 2026

1. App Data Collection

JetLagPro collects anonymous trip and survey data for ongoing research purposes. The app guides acupressure point stimulation entirely on-device; internet connectivity is used only for optional, anonymous research data submission.

What We Collect

  • Anonymous trip completion data (destination, time zones crossed, points stimulated)
  • Anonymous survey responses about jet lag symptoms and recovery
  • No personal identifiers, names, email addresses, or contact information

2. Research Survey Data Collection

We collect anonymous survey data for research purposes through our optional in-app research survey. Participation is entirely voluntary.

Anonymous Survey Data

  • Survey responses about jet lag symptoms and recovery
  • Basic demographic information (age range)

3. How We Use Information

Anonymous trip and survey data are used solely for research purposes to evaluate the effectiveness of chronoacupuncture for jet lag management.

4. Data Storage and Security

Anonymous data is securely stored in Google Firebase Firestore and cannot be linked back to individual users.

5. Third-Party Services

JetLagPro is distributed through Apple's App Store. We do not share any data with Apple beyond what is required for app distribution. Research data is stored using Google Firebase Firestore, which provides enterprise-grade security and complies with applicable data protection standards.

6. Research participation

Acupressure guidance and reminders work without research uploads. If you join the study, trip and survey data are anonymous—no name, email, or account.

When your first trip qualifies, the app asks once with Share or Not now. Share saves that trip to our research database; later qualifying trips may save automatically. Not now skips upload for that trip; the app may ask again after another qualifying trip.

Questions about research participation, or to stop new uploads after you have shared: info@jetlagpro.com. This policy is linked from the Info tab in the app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
