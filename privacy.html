Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy

Effective May 26, 2026

JetLagPro guides acupressure timing and point locations on your device. The app is informational and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment.

What we collect

Only if you opt in to anonymous research sharing:

  • Trip completion data: destination, time zones crossed, and points stimulated
  • Survey responses about jet lag symptoms and recovery

What we do not collect

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or mailing addresses
  • Accounts, logins, or contact information
  • Age, gender, or other demographic information
  • Location tracking, health records, or advertising identifiers

How we use and store data

  • Anonymous trip and survey data are used only for research on timed acupressure for jet lag management.
  • Data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore.
  • Data is not sold, used for advertising, or used for tracking.
  • Apple receives only what is required for App Store distribution; we do not send research data to Apple.

Research participation

Acupressure guidance and reminders work without uploading research data. Participation is voluntary.

  • After a qualifying trip, the app asks once: Share or Not now.
  • Share uploads that trip. Later qualifying trips may upload automatically if sharing stays on.
  • Not now skips that trip. The app may ask again after another qualifying trip.
  • In the app’s Info tab, you can turn off “Share anonymous trip data for research” to stop future uploads. Past anonymous uploads cannot be linked back to you.

Questions: info@jetlagpro.com. This policy is linked from the Info tab.
